Eucalyptus Oil for Muscle Pain
Eucalyptus oil is great for soothing sore muscles. It contains natural compounds that reduce inflammation, ease pain, and promote recovery. The cooling sensation it provides can help relieve muscle tension and soreness. To use, simply mix it with a carrier oil and massage it into the skin, or add a few drops to your bath for full-body relaxation.

Eucalyptus Oil for Asthma & Breathing Issues
Eucalyptus oil can help open your airways and make breathing easier, especially for those with asthma or congestion. It works as a natural bronchodilator, relaxing the muscles around your airways. You can inhale it through steam, use it in a diffuser, or rub it on your chest (diluted with a carrier oil). Always consult your doctor if you have asthma before use. Keep it away from pets and young children.
